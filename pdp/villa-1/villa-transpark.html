Transpark Bintaro

Jl. HR Rasuna Said No.5, Pd. Jaya, Kec. Pd. Aren, Kota Tangerang Selatan

Overview

In this project we involve as Project Planner Control for PT. Prosys Bangun Persada. Mainly involve in scheduling, project control, site coordination update, tracking progress to achieve the finish line as planned. Transpark Bintaro is the 2nd project from Trans Corp group to make a snow park inside the mall. We are managed to deliver not only the Mall but the 3 apartments as well.
