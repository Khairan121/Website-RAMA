Vishala Sidemen Retreat

Sidemen, Bali

Overview

Not only for vacation but Vishala is a new concept for retreat and staycation. Built with complex bamboo materials we involved as Project Advisor for owner representative. Coordination with architect in the planning progress including pre phase concept, site planning, and evaluate price estimate. We are collaborating with Architect Studio WNA.

Architect

Crafted by Studio WNA, the retreat embodies refined simplicity—merging Balinese cultural essence with serene architectural expressions that celebrate the natural beauty of Sidemen.

Services

Our role as Project Advisor and Project Planner focused on developing a clear project roadmap, optimizing resources, and maintaining design integrity from concept to realization.

Client

A private client seeking to create a tranquil sanctuary surrounded by Bali’s lush highland landscapes.
